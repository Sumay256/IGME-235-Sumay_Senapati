Deckflip Dungeon - Documentation

Game Overview

Deckflip Dungeon is a simple card matching game with RPG elements. Players flip cards to reveal matches and progress through levels while managing health (HP) and shields.

Controls

Click the "Start Game" button to begin.

Click on cards to reveal them and then click on a second card to attempt a match.

If a match is made, the cards are cleared and if the cards are a special type their effects are applied.

If no match is made, the cards are flipped back over and if either of the cards was an enemy, damage is applied.

Game Mechanics

  • HP: Health points; increases when HP cards are matched. Max HP is also increased by 5 permanently each match.
  • Shield: Negates complete damage from enemy cards; consumes 1 shield per dmg negation. Gives a permanent Dmg reduction stack (10%) Has a max stack limit of 5.
  • Enemies: Grunt and Brute cards deal damage when mismatched. (Grunt deals 2 flat damage and Brute deals 5)
  • Levels: Completing all matches advances the player to the next level, increasing enemy damage.
  • Buffs: Temporary notifications for HP or Shield gains appear above the grid.
  • Inert number cards just have to be matched to clear them from the board.

Development Notes

  • Cards are created dynamically using the DOM, which allows the grid to change every level.
  • The HP bar is implemented using a simple inner div. Its width updates based on the player's current HP.
  • Buffs like "HP+" and "Shield+" appear temporarily above the grid whenever the player gains a bonus. These are added dynamically to the DOM as well.
  • Used setTimeout to delay actions like hiding matched cards or flipping back mismatched cards. This creates a smooth visual effect and prevents the player from clicking too quickly.
  • The deck includes special cards (HP, Shield, enemies) and inert number cards, which are shuffled randomly each level to keep the game fresh. The deck is created and shuffled each level.
