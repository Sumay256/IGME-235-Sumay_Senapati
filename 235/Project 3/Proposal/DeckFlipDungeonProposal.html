Deckflip Dungeon

A Roguelike Memory-Card Adventure

Overview

Deckflip Dungeon is a fusion of a classic memory-matching game and a light roguelike. Players navigate a grid of facedown cards, flipping two at a time while managing limited health, abilities, and hazards. Matching pairs clears obstacles, reveals loot, and moves the player deeper into the dungeon.

  • Permanent upgrades available between runs.

Project Goals

This project aims to enhance the basic memory-game formula with:

  • Stylized presentation and thematic consistency.
  • Replayability through procedural elements.
  • A clear balance between strategy, luck, and progression.
