Dig Dug

Publishing Info

  • Published by: Atari, Inc.
  • Developed by: Namco Limited
  • Released: April, 1982

Description


Dig Dug is a 1-2 player arcade game in which you use your shovel to dig through the earth.
Stopping you are two monsters: Pooka and Fygar.
Your only weapon is an air pump.

Game Categories

  • Genre: Action
  • Perspective: Side view
  • Visual: Fixed
  • Gameplay: Arcade
